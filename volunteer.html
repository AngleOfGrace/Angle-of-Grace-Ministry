Join the Team

Volunteering is not only a way to help others, but it's also a way to enrich your own life. You'll gain new skills, meet new people and make a positive impact in your community. Take the first step today by filling the form below and see what a difference you can make!

By filling the form below, you're taking the first step towards joining a community of peoplewho are dedicated to making a difference in the world. You'll be able to use your skills and talents to help others and you'll be part of a team that's committed to making a positive impact.


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering.

As an Usher, you'll be one of the person people see when they enter the church. You'll be a warm and welcoming presence, making everyone feel at home. You'll help people find their seats, answer any questions they may have, and make sure everyone has what they need to have a meaningful worship experience.

Kids Church.

At our kids Church, we believe that learning about God should be fun! We have a wide range of age-appropriate activities that are both educational and entertaining. From story time and crafts to games and music, there's something for everyone.

Music Team.

Join our music team and experience the joy of singing and praising God with a community of like-minded people. Our Choir is open to all, regardless of musical experience. We'll provide training and support to help you grow as a singer and worship leader.

Intercessory Team.

Our intercessory Team is an essential part of our church family. Through Prayer, we believe we can move mountains and impact our community. No matter your level of prayer experience, you can be a part of this special team and help others through the power of prayer

Sanctuary Keepers.

As a sanctuary keeper, you'll have the opportunity to make a meaningful difference in the lives of opthers. You'll help to maintain a clean, orderly and welcoming environment for worship. This is a vital role in the church and we'll provide training and support to help you succeed. If you have the heart and desire to make difference, this is the team for you!

Media Team.

If you're interested in using technology to enhance worship, our media team is the perfect fit for you. You'll be responsible for operating the sound and video systems and you'll pay an important role in creating an engaging and meaningful worship experience.

Welfare Department.

The welfare department is a compassionate and caring team that helps to meet the needs of those who are struggling. Whether it's providing food, clothing or other assistance, this team is dedicated to showing the love of Christ to those who need it most.

Technical Department.

The technical department is vital to smooth operation of our church. This team is responsible for keeping our faculties running smoothly and making repairs as needed. Whether it's fixing a leaky faucet or replacing a light bulb, this team is dedicated to maintaining a safe and functional environment for our congregation.
