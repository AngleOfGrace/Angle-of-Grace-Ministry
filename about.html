We Are Angle of Grace

We are a church that is passionate about helping people encounter God and experience transformation. We believe that everyone has a purpose and a place in the body of Christ and we're committed to helping people discover and fulfill that purpose.
Our Church is made up of people from all walks of life and we welcome everyone with open arms. We would love for you to join us on this journey of faith. Come and see what god has in store for you!

Our Purpose.

Our church exists to glorify God and make disciples of Jesus Christ. We do this by helping people come to know Christ, grow in their faith, and serve others in love. We believe that everyone has a part to play in God's kingdom and we are committed to equipping and empowering people to use their gifts and talents to further God's work in the world. Come and be a part of what God is doing here!

Our Mission.

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who are equipped and empowered to be salt and light in the world. We seek to accomplish this by sharing the gospel, teaching God's Word, and living lives that reflect Christ's love. We believe that every person has worth and dignity, and we desire to see people grow in their faith and become all that God created them to be.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Meet Our Team.

Rev. Calvary Uhunamure General Overseer

Reverend Calvary Uhunamure is a passionate and gifted teacher of God's Word. He has a heart for people and a desire to see them grow in their relationship with God.
His sermons are relevant, thought-provoking and challenging and he is committed to helping people apply the Word of God to their everyday lives. His warm and approachable personality makes him a favourite with church members of all ages.

Pst. Rose Uhunamure (Mrs.) Assistant Pastor

Pastor Mrs. Rose Uhunamure is an energetic and compassionate leader. As an Assistant Pastor, she plays a vital role in the life of the Church, serving as a shepherd and teacher to the congregation.
She has a gift for building relationship and helping people connect with God and each other.

Pst. Abel Youth Pastor

Pastor Abel is a caring and thoughtful person who has a heart for people and a passion for God's Word. He is known for his gentle and wise counsel and his ability to connect with people on a personal level.

Jacob believe Assistant Youth Pastor

Master Believe is a fun and energetic leader who is passionate about working with young people. He helps to oversee the youth ministry and provides a positive, godly influence in lives of young people.

Calvary Davidson Youth President

Master Davidson is a talented and hardworking leader who serves with all diligence and humility. He is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the youth ministry, including leadership development, outreach and discipleship.
